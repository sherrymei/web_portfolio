ABOUT ME


Hi. I'm Sherry Mei, living in Boston, MA. I recently graduated from Boston College with a computer science degree in May 2017. I enjoy writing code for applications, whether that be software, mobile, or web. My strongest programming language is Java. Utilizing my problem solving, analytical, critical thinking, and creative skills are what I am best at. Hopefully, a full-time position as an application developer will enhance these skills.

I love writing code while listening to music; it keeps me productive and focused. During my leisure time, I enjoy not only exploring novel places but also spending quality time with friends and family.

Currently, as a side project, I am developing an Android application called Reminders. This app is useful for users who forget easily; users can add items they want to be reminded, such as favorite quotes, to do lists, business cards, etc.
